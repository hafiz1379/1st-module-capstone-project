FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022

The 2022 FIFA World Cup was held in Qatar from November 21st to December 18th, 2022. It featured 32 teams playing in eight stadiums across five host cities, with the final held at the newly completed Lusail Iconic Stadium. The tournament aimed to promote football in the Middle East and bring people together in a celebration of sport and culture.

FIFA World Cup 2022 Logo

The FIFA World Cup 2022 logo was inspired by the traditional woollen shawls worn during the winter months in Qatar.
It features a design that represents the infinity symbol and the number eight, which is a reference to the eight stadiums that hosted matches.
The logo is intended to embody the tournament's theme of 'Unity' and symbolize the connections between the event, the host nation, and the world.
